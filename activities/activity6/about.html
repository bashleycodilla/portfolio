OUR STORY!

I Bashley Rovidel V. Codilla, the owner of Chicken Tenders ni Bash started Chicken Tenders ni Bash because I wanted chicken tenders that were very affordable for the people, juicy inside, and full of flavor. I experimented in my home in Minglanilla, sharing batches with my family and friends that I invited inside the house, and their smiles showed me that good food can brighten anyone’s day. Now, every Chicken Tender we make carries that same goal: to share a piece of my chicken tenders and bring joy in every bite that feels the bond of family and friends.

Bashley Rovidel V. Codilla

Founder and CEO
